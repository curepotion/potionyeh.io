IDC OVERSEER (UIUX Design)

ABOUT THE PROJECT

During my tenure at Vertex as a UX Designer, I embarked on an innovative project, focused on crafting an app for monitoring servers for field operators and supervisers and display collected data over time in a chart view. Our mission was multifaceted: not only were we to consider the environmental implications, but we also had to judiciously choose the optimal technological stack from front-end to back-end.

One of the pivotal aspects of this project was developing 'dev-ready' mockups. This entailed creating detailed and precise representations of the final product, ensuring that our developers had a clear blueprint to follow. These mockups were not just static visuals; they encapsulated interactive elements, transitions, and user flows. Feedback loops were integral at this stage. Regular sessions with the development team ensured that design intentions were accurately translated into functional elements.
